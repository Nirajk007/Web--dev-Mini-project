Paneer Butter Masala

Ingredients: Paneer, tomato puree, butter, cream, spices.

Steps: Cook spices in butter, add puree, simmer with cream, add paneer and serve with naan.

Masala Dosa

Ingredients: Dosa batter, potatoes, onions, mustard seeds, curry leaves.

Steps: Make dosa on tawa, stuff with spiced mashed potato filling and serve with chutney.

Chole Bhature

Ingredients: Chickpeas, onion, tomato, bhature dough, spices.

Steps: Boil chickpeas, prepare masala, mix together. Deep fry bhature and serve hot.

Gulab Jamun

Ingredients: Milk powder, maida, sugar syrup, cardamom.

Steps: Knead dough, make small balls, fry till golden, soak in sugar syrup.
